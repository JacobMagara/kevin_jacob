Project Overview

Disease Tracker is a web application designed to monitor and analyze outbreaks of infectious diseases. The platform allows users to view recent outbreaks, track vaccination campaigns, and input new data related to incidents and vaccinations.

Mission

The primary goal of Disease Tracker is to assist public health officials, researchers, and the general public in understanding the spread of infectious diseases. By providing up-to-date information on disease outbreaks and vaccination efforts, the platform aims to support informed decision-making and promote public health.

Key Features

  • Track recent outbreaks and view detailed data on cases and deaths.
  • Monitor vaccination campaigns and analyze their impact.
  • Add new incident and vaccination records to keep data current.

Data Sources

Data used in Disease Tracker is sourced from various public health databases, government health departments, and international organizations such as the World Health Organization (WHO). The platform aggregates this data to provide comprehensive and reliable information.
